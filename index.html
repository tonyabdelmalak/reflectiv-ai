Ethics, Privacy & Governance

Data privacy & security

  • PHI off by default; synthetic or de-identified training data.
  • BAA controls when PHI is enabled; encryption, RBAC, purge on expiration.
  • Vendor risk management and right-to-audit.

Algorithmic fairness

  • Pre-deployment bias tests and periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance, publish remediation steps.
  • Guardrails block non-clinical proxies for targeting.

Transparency

  • Reason codes in recommendations.
  • Model cards document data sources and limits.
  • AI-generated content clearly labeled.

Human oversight

  • Humans curate scenarios and rubrics with escalation paths.
  • All message libraries versioned for MLR traceability.
