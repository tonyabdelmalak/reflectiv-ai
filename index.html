Ethics, Privacy & Governance

Data privacy & security
  • PHI is off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When enabled, BAA + encryption in transit/at rest + RBAC + purge on expiration.
  • Vendor risk management with annual reviews and right-to-audit.
Informed consent
  • Customers obtain explicit consent and provide opt-out when real data is used.
Algorithmic bias & fairness
  • Pre-deployment bias testing and periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance with remediation steps.
Transparency & explainability
  • Recommendations include reason codes and model cards.
  • AI-generated content is clearly labeled.
Human oversight & accountability
  • Humans curate scenarios and rubrics; escalation paths defined.
  • All message libraries versioned for MLR traceability.
